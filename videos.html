مجلة إلادارة والتسويق الرقمي

Journal of Management and Digital Marketing

مجلة اإلدارة والتسويق الرقمي هي منبر أكاديمي مرموق يسعى إلى تعزيز التفاعل بين مجتمع األبحاث والمهتمين بمجاالت اإلدارة والتسويق الرقمي. تعتبر هذه المجلة بوابة للمفاهيم الرائدة واألفكار المبتكرة في علوم اإلدارة والتسويق الرقمي، و تستضيف المجلة مقاالت بحثية أصلية ومراجعات نقدية تلقى الضوء على التطورات الحديثة واالتجاهات في هذين القطاعين. و تركز المجلة على مواضيع متنوعة تشمل إدارة المؤسسات، واستراتيجيات التسويق الرقمي، وتكنولوجيا المعلومات، واالبتكار في التسويق، وتحليل البيانات، وسالسل التوريد الرقمية، و تهدف المجلة إلى تعزيز الفهم العميق والشامل للتحديات والفرص التي تطرأ في هذا السياق المتطور باستمرار، و بفضل التنوع في المواضيع التي تغطيها، ُتعد "مجلة اإلدارة والتسويق الرقمي" مصدرًا قيمًا للباحثين واألكاديميين والمحترفين الذين يسعون لفهم التحوالت الحديثة في ميدان اإلدارة والتسويق في عصر التكنولوجيا الرقمية.

The Journal of Management and Digital Marketing covers a diverse range of fields that are integral to the realms of management and digital marketing. Here are key areas and topics that the journal may encompass:

Strategic Management:

Research and studies related to formulating and implementing strategies that enable organizations to achieve their goals and objectives effectively

Marketing Management:

Examination of marketing strategies, market research, product development, pricing, distribution, and branding strategies to enhance organizational performance.

Digital Marketing:

Exploration of various aspects of digital marketing, including social media marketing, content marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), online advertising, and e-commerce strategies.

Consumer Behavior:

Studies on understanding consumer preferences, decision-making processes, and the factors influencing consumer choices in the digital era.

E-Marketing

Research focusing on electronic marketing methods, online advertising, email marketing, and the use of digital channels to reach and engage consumers.

Data Analytics in Marketing

Exploration of data-driven approaches for marketing decision-making, including the analysis of big data, customer analytics, and predictive modeling.

Brand Management

Analysis of brand development, brand positioning, and strategies for building and managing strong brands in the digital landscape.

Supply Chain Management

Research on optimizing the processes involved in the production and distribution of goods and services, with a focus on digital tools and technologies.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Studies on building and maintaining strong relationships with customers through effective communication, personalized services, and customer loyalty programs.

Social Media Management

Exploration of strategies for leveraging social media platforms for brand promotion, customer engagement, and reputation management.

Digital Innovation

Examination of innovative technologies and practices that drive digital transformation in marketing and management processes.

Entrepreneurship and Startups

Research on the challenges and opportunities for entrepreneurs and startups in the digital age, including digital marketing strategies for new ventures.

Online Consumer Reviews and Reputation Management

Analysis of the impact of online reviews and strategies for managing and enhancing organizational reputation in the digital space.

E-commerce and Online Retailing

Studies on the trends, challenges, and opportunities in the digital retail sector, including online marketplaces and consumer behavior in online shopping.
